How to use wallpapers
---
- If you wish to download any of these wallpapers, make sure to put them in `~/Pictures/Wallpapers` directory, so they can be recognized by whe wallpaper picker
- To apply the wallpaper of your choice, press `Mod Key + Shift + W` and choose a wallpaper
